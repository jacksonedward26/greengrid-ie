How it runs

A four-step sequence, start to finish

  1. 01

    Scope

    We define the electrical scope against the wider project — programme, budget, and the standards it needs to meet.

  2. 02

    Design

    Engineering design and load calculations are worked out and checked for compliance before anything is ordered or installed.

  3. 03

    Manage

    We coordinate contractors and track progress against programme, staying involved through installation rather than handing off after design.

  4. 04

    Commission

    Testing, commissioning, and sign-off, with handover documentation delivered at the end.
